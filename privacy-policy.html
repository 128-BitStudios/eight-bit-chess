Privacy Policy for 8bit Chess Puzzles

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

128 Bit Studios ("we", "our", or "us") operates the 8bit Chess Puzzles mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our service:

  • Game Progress Data: We store your game progress, completed puzzles, scores, and achievements locally on your device.
  • Purchase Information: When you make in-app purchases, we process transactions through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.
  • Device Information: We may collect anonymous device information for app analytics and crash reporting.

How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Save your game progress and preferences
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Provide customer support

Data Storage

All game progress data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store your personal game data on external servers.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements (only for non-premium users). AdMob may collect and use data for ad personalization. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple App Store: For processing in-app purchases

Children's Privacy

Our app is rated 4+ and suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not require any personal information to play.

Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your information. Game data is stored securely on your device using industry-standard methods.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • Disable personalized ads in your device settings
  • Purchase the premium version to remove all advertisements

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: 128bit.studios@gmail.com

Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • Apple App Store Guidelines
